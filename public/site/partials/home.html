A Free Tool for Creating and Maintaining a Shared Database For Communities, Linguists and Language Learners

Share

Collaboration

Speakers, linguists, and learners can contribute remotely to one centralized database, distributing the responsibility of documentation.

Access

The database is shared between researchers and the community. Everyone with permission has access to the data, ensuring that language information is not just on the computers of a few people.

Security

While it is easy to share data, the database is secure. Permission settings control who can see what.
Search

Customize

LingSync is customizable. You decide what is important and how much detail goes into your database.

Search

Data is easy to tag, search, and sort (under development) by factors you can choose (i.e., speaker, date, morpheme or construction type).

Import / Export

It is very easy to import and export data in a variety of formats (under development).
Revitalize

Participate

Learners can participate in recording and transcribing their language. Users can easily search the database for specific words or phrases, and discover patterns on their own.

Learn

Material can be converted from LingSync to language lessons on a complementary language learning app (see example).
Features

Free & Accessible

LingSync is free and open source. It is also a web app, which means it works in any desktop or mobile browser.

Safe

LingSync automatically backs up your database. Data history is accessible, making it easy to go back and undo mistakes.

Smart

LingSync learns from your data to streamline morpheme glossing.

Customizable

LingSync is customizable. You decide what is important and how much detail goes into your database.

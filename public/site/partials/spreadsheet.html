Spreadsheet

Please note that the Spreadsheet app is no longer supported.

  • Quick and Easy Data Entry: The clean interface makes it quick and easy to enter data. Suited for linguistic field methods classes, as well as for building databases for group research projects.
  • Customizable Data Fields: Add and modify data fields according to your project's needs.
  • Audio Data: Upload audio files (.mp3, .wav), or record audio directly to the app.
  • Semi-automatic Glosser: Learns morphemes and glosses from your data. Helps streamline morpheme glossing.
  • Search & Export: Restrictive search narrows down your search from previous search results. Search results are exportable to plain text or WordPress glossed format, to be used in handouts and blogs.

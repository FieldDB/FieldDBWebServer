Spreadsheet

  • Quick and Easy Data Entry: The LingSync Spreadsheet app has a clean user interface which makes data entry quick and easy. It is suitable for linguistic field methods classes.
  • Customizable Data Fields: You can add and modify data fields according to what your project requires.
  • Audio Data: You can record your audio data directly to the app. You can also upload audio files (.mp3, .wav).
  • Semi-automatic Glosser: The app's semi-auto glosser learns morphemes and glosses from your data and helps streamline morpheme glossing.
  • Search and Export: Narrow-down search targets the data you are looking for. Search results are exportable to simple text or wordpress glossed format to be used in handouts and blogs.
  • One Database for Both Apps: One database underlies both Spreadsheet and Dative apps. You can choose either app to access your data depending on your purpose. For quick data entry, you can use the Spreadsheet app. To investigate your data more thoroughly, you can use the Dative app.

Warning

We will be shutting down LingSync.org on August 1, 2017, after which time corpora will be read-only.

2015 Fall semester field methods classes have notified us of many problems in LingSync and we do not recommend using LingSync for future classes.

If you already have an account and corpora on LingSync, you still have access to your existing data until August 1, 2017 (after which time the corpora will be read-only). Please note, however, that we have disabled new user registration and new corpus creation on the app, and that we are no longer fixing the bugs.

If you were planning on using LingSync for your classes next semester, please email info@lingsync.org for instructions on how to use Dative.
